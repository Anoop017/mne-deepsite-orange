About MNE Components

Established in 2019, MNE Components India Pvt Ltd has grown to become a leading manufacturer of battery connectors and terminals in the Indian market.

With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and a dedicated team of professionals, we deliver products that meet the highest industry standards.
